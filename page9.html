Christopher Archer is the Founder and Managing Partner at Archer, Archer and Co.
Christopher has significant experience in negotiating, reviewing and drafting transaction contracts, structuring fund offerings for international and local investors and advising on appropriate strategies for transactions in the international and local capital markets. He consults on a variety of areas and also provides support services for several Ghanaian companies and foreign investors in Ghana in the areas of incorporation, foreign investment, commercial law, insurance and debt recovery.
Prior to founding Archer, Archer and Co. Christopher was a Senior Associate at Heward-Mills & Co. an Accra based law firm.
Christopher has additional responsibility for the continuing Legal Education of all Members of the firm.
In WHO’S WHO LEGAL (Ghana) 2015 published by Law Business Research Ltd of the United Kingdom he is listed as one of twenty-four leading practitioners in the corporate law field in Ghana and described as having a “stellar reputation” in this area with a “meticulous approach” in complex corporate transactions. In the area of finance Christopher is listed as one of twenty-one of the best finance practitioners in the country and described as “extremely dedicated to his Clients and provides hands-on strategic advice”.
